What is the Holy Grail?

The Holy Grail is a legendary object in Arthurian literature, often depicted as a cup or chalice that grants eternal life or sustenance.

History

The concept of the Holy Grail has evolved over centuries, with various interpretations in religious, literary, and popular culture.

In Popular Culture

The Holy Grail has been referenced in numerous films, books, and songs, often symbolizing the pursuit of a great and unattainable goal.
